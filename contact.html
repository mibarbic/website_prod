About @micanbar



The name @micanbar was conceived during an early 2000's existential crisis when Twitter and other media giants were on the rise.
In the fear that he would not get a cool handle, Michelangelo came up with micanbar.

mi and can are from "Michelangelo", while bar is from his last name "Barbic".
Michelangelo has a huge love for industrial architecture, computer hardware, EDM, and Japanese culture.
Fun fact: micanbar by chance translates to tangerine bar in Japanese, "ミカンバー"
Because of this, in his projects you may see micanOS (tangerineOS), a sub-brand of micanbar associated with an stylized logo of a tangerine.
Newsom Computing is a subsidary of micanbar and micanOS.
